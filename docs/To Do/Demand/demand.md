# Macroeconomic Demand

## The Classical Definition
In the classical economic framework defined by Henry Dunning Macleod, **Demand** is the union of two distinct properties:
1.  **Desire:** The abstract, emotional willingness to acquire an Economic Quantity.
2.  **Power to Purchase:** The actual liquid property (Cash + Rights of Action) available to exchange for it.

*   *Demand is only effective when Desire is backed by the Power to Purchase.*
